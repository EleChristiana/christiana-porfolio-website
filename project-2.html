Project Overview

Project Name: MedRemind
Description: MedRemind is a web-based health management application built with Angular, designed to help HIV patients consistently follow their medication schedules. It provides users with digital tools to manage medications, set reminders, monitor their health, and access vital information—improving self-care and health outcomes.

Key Features:
Medication Schedule Setup: Patients can add medications with custom dosage instructions and reminder intervals.
Real-Time Notifications: Built-in reminder system using alarms and alerts to ensure timely medication intake.
Progress & Adherence Tracking: Users can check off doses as “taken” or “missed” and view their adherence history.
Appointment Reminders: Schedule and get notified of upcoming hospital visits and checkups.
Health Logging: Daily logs for symptoms or side effects, giving users and caregivers a clearer view of overall wellness.
