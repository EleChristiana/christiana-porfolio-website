PlantIQ

PlantIQ is a pioneering platform aimed at transforming agriculture in Nigeria. Addressing food shortages, PlantIQ empowers everyone—from working professionals to local farmers—to grow their own food and adopt modern agricultural practices.

Live Link

Project Overview

Project Name: PlantIQ
Objective: To revolutionize agriculture in Nigeria by empowering individuals and local farmers to grow food sustainably using modern techniques and digital tools.
Project Description: PlantIQ is a digital platform developed to promote self-sufficient, tech-enabled agriculture among Nigerians. It provides practical resources for home gardening, educational content for modern farming, and digital tools for crop analysis and farm management.

Key Features: Home Gardening Guide: Step-by-step crop cultivation instructions, downloadable as PDFs for offline use.
Educational Channel: Expert farming videos integrated via YouTube API.
Plant Analysis Tool: Image upload feature for identifying plants and receiving care tips (powered by Gemini API, in development).
