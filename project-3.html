Project Overview

The e-commerce pressure cooker website is a user-friendly platform designed for both shopping and culinary exploration. It allows users to browse and purchase pressure cookers with ease while also accessing a variety of international recipes through TheMealDB API. Users can search recipes by country and view detailed cooking instructions, ingredient lists, and images. The product page includes features like product search, detailed views, and an interactive shopping cart that supports quantity adjustments, item removal, and direct checkout access. Secure payments are facilitated via PayPal or credit/debit card, with instant purchase confirmation and delivery date notification. Additionally, the homepage integrates YouTube cooking videos, offering tutorials and tips on pressure cooker use, powered by a YouTube channel endpoint.
